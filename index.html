Temperature Conversion

Enter the temperature in degrees Celsius (°C) in the input box, then click the convert button and you will get the conversion result in Fahrenheit (°F)

Formula

To convert Celsius to Fahrenheit, you can use the following formula: Fahrenheit = (Celsius × 9/5) + 32 To convert a temperature from Celsius to Fahrenheit, multiply the Celsius value by 9/5 and then add 32. The resulting value will be the equivalent temperature in Fahrenheit.
